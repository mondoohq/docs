Run Mondoo reports to share the security and compliance information that is essential to your business. Mondoo provides two types of reports:

- [Export your data](/platform/reporting/export/) to a .csv file that you can import into the software of your choice, such as business intelligence or spreadsheet management tools.

- [Generate a compliance report](/platform/compliance/monitor/progress/#generate-a-compliance-report) to provide evidence to your auditor.
